Embedded systems are specialized computing systems designed to perform dedicated functions or tasks within larger systems.
Unlike general-purpose computers, embedded systems are typically embedded into other devices and are dedicated to specific applications, often with real-time computing constraints.
These systems can be found in a wide range of devices and industries, such as consumer electronics, automotive systems, medical devices, industrial machines, and more.
